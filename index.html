Features

Epic Story

Journey into a shattered kingdom to uncover the truth. What happened before the orc invasion that left everything in ruins? Follow King Corwin and his loyal lords as they fight to save their land and restore hope to a broken realm.

Intense Combat

Engage in thrilling battles featuring unique skills, powerful enemies, and intricate strategies, all controlled with intuitive one-finger simplicity.

Build and Defend

Fortify your kingdom by constructing key structures, from training soldiers in the Barracks to reinforcing your defenses with towering Walls, preparing for the chaos ahead.

Command Your Soldiers

Take direct control of your trained soldiers and lead them into battle. Issue commands like Follow, Attack, and Hold Ground to strategize and outmaneuver powerful foes. Shape the battlefield with your leadership and tactical skill.

Explore Dungeons for Riches

Venture into treacherous dungeons filled with danger and treasure. Uncover rare loot and discover powerful weapons to enhance your strength and prepare for the battles ahead.
